1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Project Verba collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our services, including our website, mobile application, and loyalty program (collectively, the "Services").

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and password
  • Profile Information: Additional details like profile picture, preferences, and interests
  • Transaction Information: Purchase details, payments, and loyalty program activities
  • Communication Information: Customer service inquiries and feedback
  • Survey Information: Responses to surveys, questionnaires, or feedback forms

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you use our Services, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address
  • Usage Information: Pages visited, features used, time spent on Services
  • Location Information: General location based on IP address
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: Information collected through tracking technologies

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:

  • Business partners and loyalty program merchants
  • Social media platforms you connect to our Services
  • Third-party data providers for demographic information
  • Public sources that help us serve you better

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Providing and Improving Services

  • Operating and maintaining our Services
  • Processing transactions and managing your loyalty account
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries
  • Personalizing your experience and providing relevant content
  • Developing new features and improving existing Services

3.2 Communication

  • Sending account-related communications
  • Providing promotional offers and marketing communications
  • Sending loyalty program updates and reward notifications
  • Conducting surveys and requesting feedback

3.3 Security and Compliance

  • Protecting against fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our terms
  • Resolving disputes and investigating violations
  • Maintaining records for business and legal purposes

4. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 With Your Consent

We share information when you explicitly consent to such sharing.

4.2 Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers including payment processors, cloud storage providers, marketing services, and customer support platforms.

4.3 Business Partners

We may share information with loyalty program partners and merchants to process transactions, provide promotions, and enable cross-platform benefits.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law, court orders, or to protect our rights and prevent fraud.

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Employee training on data protection practices
  • Incident response procedures

Important: No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our Services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Retention periods depend on:

  • The type of information collected

  • Legal and regulatory requirements
  • Your account status and activity level

When information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.
